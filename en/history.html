Milestones
Milestones
2018

November

  • Assigned three groups of engineers to Nervión Industries in Spain for technology transfer, including: industrial design, manufacturing process, project management, industrial safety management and Sandblasting & Coating…etc.
  • Signed a contract with Ørsted on suppling 56 jacket foundations, this is our very first order, and it is the first time Ørsted orders over 40 jacket foundations from one single supplier in the world.
  • In order to increase our own certificated welders, we have entrusted the Yunlin-Chiayi-Tainan Regional Branch and the Kaohsiung-Pintung-Penghu-Taitung Regional Branch of Workforce Development Agencies to implement welders’ vocational education programs, and recruited total of 44 trainees

September

  • Entrusted the Center for Regional Industry Academia Collaboration, National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology to hold the first recruitment session for employees. The written examination was conducted on 14 September 2018, followed by interview sessions during 5 to 6 of November 2018. The admission list was announced on 12 November 2018, and new employees have reported for duty by groups on 3 December 2018.

July

  • CSC assigned Mr. Ya-Chou Wang as Director of the Board, to substitute Director Ching-Chung Cheng for his unexpired term. The 1st Board of Directors of SDMS elected Director Wang as Chairman in the 4th Board Meeting.

June

  • Signed the training contract with Spanish subsea foundation manufacturer Nervión.
  • Ørsted visited and conducted the Mock-up TP kick-off meeting with SDMS, which was the very first project for SDMS.
  • SDMS, represented by President Lu, signed the LOI of Mock-up TP with Ørsted.

April

  • The 1st Board of Directors of SDMS held the 1st Board Meeting. Director Ching-Chung Cheng was elected as Chairman, and Mr. Wu-Hsiung Lu was hired as president.
  • Held the ceremony for plant construction of SDMS and industrial chain alliance.
  • Completed incorporation registration, and gained our GUI number 42816688, capital set at NT$3.421 billion dollars.

March

  • The CSC’s 16th Board of Directors adopted an investment project of NT$3.421 billion dollars on establishing an subsea foundation manufacturing company in its 16th Board Meeting, and an subsea foundation production line would be built up in the Kaohsiung Marine Technology Industrial Innovation Zone, Singda Harbor. The establishment was estimated to be completed by December, 2019.
